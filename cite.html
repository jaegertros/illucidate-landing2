Citing Work

How to cite Illucidate and data sources

Please cite both the software and the original datasets used in your analysis workflow.

Software citation

@software{illucidate2025,
  title = {Illucidate: Illuminating early signals in bacterial detection},
  author = {Waddell, Caleb L.},
  year = {2025},
  url = {https://github.com/jaegertros/illucidate},
  version = {0.1.0},
  note = {Open-source toolkit for multivariate analysis of time-series biosensor data}
}

Data citation principles

When analyzing published or externally sourced datasets, always cite the original publication and honor its license/usage terms.

If datasets came from direct author communication, keep explicit permission records and acknowledge data providers in methods or acknowledgments.

When in doubt, over-cite rather than under-cite.
