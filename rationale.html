Rationale

Why this work matters

Illucidate is built around a practical gap: rich assay data is often analyzed one channel at a time, which can hide early multivariate detection signals.

The core issue

Traditional workflows often split samples into separate confirmation pathways and treat OD, luminescence, timing, and related signals independently. That can delay useful conclusions even when the data already contains earlier combined patterns.

Illucidate focuses on multivariate, time-structured analysis so relationships between measurements can be seen sooner and interpreted more clearly.

About the name

Illucidate combines Illuminate and Elucidate: bring signals to light and make their meaning clear.
